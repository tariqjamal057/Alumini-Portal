About Us

Karpagam Institute of Technology is one of the top Private Engineering colleges in India with the mission of providing excellent technical education to the students of engineering with dynamic faculty and the state-of-art facilities.

  • The private engineering college is equipped with latest technological developments and provides the best platform for the engineering students to become independent thinkers and employable by the top companies.
  • KIT, one of the best colleges in Coimbatore, India offers a dynamic atmosphere for the students of engineering to learn the best of the technologies and apply the learning in their personal life.
  • The percentage of students getting placed in top companies increases every year.The engineering students of our college are regularly involved in extra-curricular activities and have made their mark with top performances in many academic and sporting events in the recent years.
  • KIT, one of the best Engineering colleges in Coimbatore provides all the support for the college students to participate and gain top acclamations in various events across the country.
